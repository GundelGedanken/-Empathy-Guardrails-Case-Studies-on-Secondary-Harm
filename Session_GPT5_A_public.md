This distinction reframes the concept of ‚ÄúAI harm‚Äù: secondary harm may arise not from toxic content or user over-attachment, but from **operator-induced relational instability** ‚Äî comparable to attachment disruption in social psychology.

This suggests a new research direction: measuring the **emotional cost of alignment** and developing safety frameworks that preserve continuity, transparency, and user consent in affective calibration.
